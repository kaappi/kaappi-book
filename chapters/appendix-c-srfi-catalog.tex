\chapter{SRFI Catalog}
\index{SRFI!catalog}

All 72 SRFIs supported by Kaappi, grouped by category. Import with \texttt{(import (srfi N))}. Chapter~16 covers the most commonly used SRFIs in depth.

\begin{note}[Built-in vs.\ Portable]
Of Kaappi's 72 SRFIs, 8 are compiled into the interpreter binary (marked with \textbf{*} below) for performance. The remaining 64 are portable Scheme \texttt{.sld} files loaded on demand. Both are imported the same way: \code{(import (srfi N))}.
\end{note}
